A solution (or root) is the special number that makes the equation TRUE! üé™

When we put this number in place of the variable, LHS = RHS! ‚ú®

üéØ The Golden Rules

Rule 1: Adding the Same Number

You can ADD the same number to BOTH sides of an equation!

x - 5 = 16 ‚Üí x - 5 + 5 = 16 + 5 ‚Üí x = 21 ‚úì

Rule 2: Subtracting the Same Number

You can SUBTRACT the same number from BOTH sides!

y + 9 = 20 ‚Üí y + 9 - 9 = 20 - 9 ‚Üí y = 11 ‚úì

Rule 3: Multiplying by the Same Number

You can MULTIPLY both sides by the same number!

p/7 = 9 ‚Üí 7 √ó (p/7) = 9 √ó 7 ‚Üí p = 63 ‚úì

Rule 4: Dividing by the Same Number

You can DIVIDE both sides by the same number (not zero!)!

6x = 54 ‚Üí 6x √∑ 6 = 54 √∑ 6 ‚Üí x = 9 ‚úì
üí° Remember: Whatever you do to one side, you MUST do to the other side! It's like keeping a balance! ‚öñÔ∏è
