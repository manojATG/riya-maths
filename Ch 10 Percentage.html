üéØ Finding Percentage of Something

üé™ The Magic Formula:

x% of y = (x/100) √ó y

Convert % to fraction, then multiply!

"OF" means MULTIPLY!

üåü Step-by-Step Example:

Find 30% of 40 mangoes

Step 1: Convert 30% to fraction = 30/100

Step 2: Multiply by 40

30/100 √ó 40 = (30 √ó 40)/100 = 1200/100 = 12

Answer: 12 mangoes

Find 25% of 80 students

25% = 25/100 = 1/4

1/4 √ó 80 = 80/4 = 20

Answer: 20 students
